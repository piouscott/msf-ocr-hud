Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

MSF Counter is a browser extension that helps Marvel Strike Force players find team counters. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

Data Collection

This extension:

  • Does NOT collect personal information
  • Does NOT track your browsing activity
  • Does NOT send data to external servers (except official MSF API)
  • Stores preferences locally in your browser

MSF API Access

When you connect your MSF account, the extension accesses the official Marvel Strike Force API to retrieve your roster and game data. This data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties.

Local Storage

The extension uses browser local storage to save:

  • Your team and counter preferences
  • Cached game data for performance
  • API tokens (stored securely in browser storage)

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
